Welcome to the KIUTSO Awards

The KIUTSO Awards is the biggest and most prestigious event in Kampala International University! This is an annual event organized by the Students Government of the University, and it celebrates the achievements of the students, staff, and alumni of the University.

The KIUTSO Awards is a night of glitz, glamour, and recognition for those who have made a significant impact on the University community. The awards ceremony will feature a variety of categories that recognize excellence in academics, research, sports, arts, and leadership.

We invite you to be part of this exciting event and to join us in recognizing the achievements of our peers and colleagues. Whether you are a student, staff member, or alumni, there is a category for you. By participating in the KIUTSO Awards, you will have the opportunity to showcase your talents and accomplishments and be recognized by your peers and the University community.

The event will take place at the University's main auditorium and will be attended by students, staff, alumni, and members of the local community. The evening will feature a red carpet reception, a sit-down dinner, and an awards ceremony.

Don't miss this opportunity to be part of this historic event! Submit your nominations today and join us as we celebrate the achievements of the Kampala International University community.

We look forward to seeing you at the KIUTSO Awards!
